About Our Company

We’re a registered Sole Proprietorship business located in Jaipur. We’re ancestral goldsmiths working on jewllery manufacturing from last last fourteen years or from 2007. We manufactures different types of jewellery as per customer’s need.

We’have staff of more than 20 people, continuously working day & night on manufacturing jewellery with dedication to make the best and unique style of jewelleries in this modern era.

We aim to deliver high quality & different designs of jewlleries, yet at affordable pricing. Our designing team creates a blend of contemporary and traditional designs with creativity and modernity at its heart.
