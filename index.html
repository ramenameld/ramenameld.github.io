We build trust

We do not only manufacture precious jewellery but also build a strong relationship and trust with our customers, that keep us connected with them for a long time.

It makes us graceful

The custom of wearing jewellery is from very old times. Jewellery has been adorning our ancestors, mothers/sisters and gods/goddesses etc. since ages. Wearing jewellery make us more beautiful.

Ancestral

We are ancestral goldsmiths, the jewellery we make has been making in our family for many generations, we have inherited this art, which we have been doing very well and honestly.

Working since ages

We have been working on rural jewellery for hundreds of years and on national and international jewellery designs for almost two centuries.

Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ's)


  • Do we have a store ?

    No, we don't have a store, we have workshop, we manufacture jewellery and do order-based work.

  • Benefit of working with us ?

    If you get the jewellery manufactured from us, you will save a lot of margin in this.

  • How do we work ?

    First of all, jewellery designer make design of jewellery on paper, after this we make base of jewellery using metal, then we make ghat(base) from it, then we add other required materials to make it beautiful.

  • Which type of materials we use ?

    We use finest quality of metals, 100% real & pure Gold, Diamond, Ruby etc.
